Community Guidelines 

By using Pixtery, you agree to these guidelines and our Terms of Use. Don’t submit copyrighted material you don’t have the rights to, nudity, graphic violence, or content that is otherwise hateful or illegal in the United States. Violation of these guidelines may result in deleted content, disabled accounts, or other legal action.

  • Only share content that you have the right to share.

You own the content you share on Pixtery. Don’t share anything you’ve copied or collected from the Internet that you don’t have the right to share.

  • Be conscientious of what you create and share.

We do not allow graphic sexual content  or content that does or appears to sexually exploit or endanger children on Pixtery.

Anyone with a link to your Pixtery can share it with others and access it, including if they don’t have an account, so only send it to people you trust. Your display name will be shown with your Pixtery.

  • Don’t impersonate others.

You don’t have to use your real name on Pixtery, but you may not impersonate others or create accounts for the purpose of violating our guidelines.

  • Follow the law.

Pixtery is not a place to support or praise terrorism, organized crime, or hate groups. Offering sexual services, buying or selling firearms, alcohol, and tobacco products between private individuals, and buying or selling non-medical or pharmaceutical drugs are also not allowed. 

We have zero tolerance for sharing sexual content involving minors or threatening to post intimate images of others.

  • Respect other members of the Pixtery community.

You may not encourage violence or attack anyone based on their race, ethnicity, national origin, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, religious affiliation, disabilities, or diseases. 

Threats of harm to public and personal safety aren't allowed. This includes specific threats of physical harm as well as threats of theft, vandalism, and other financial harm. 

The Daily Pixtery can be viewed by everyone. We reserve the right to reject a Daily Pixtery submission or later remove an accepted Daily Pixtery submission, for any reason, whether it is due to noncompliance with these Community Guidelines, or for any other reasons. Failure to follow these Community Guidelines may result in deactivation of your account.

  • Do not glorify self-injury.

You may not encourage or urge people to self-injure, nor attack, harass, or ridicule victims or survivors of self-injury..

 

Thank you for using Pixtery and abiding by these guidelines.,

The Pixtery Team
